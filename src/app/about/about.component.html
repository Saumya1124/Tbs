A Tablespoon Of Spook was born out of sheer love for all the scary things based on Spook Baby's real life incidents and imagination. Exposed to horror genre at a very early age of three, Spook Baby acquired an undying love for this particular genre. Where movies like conjuring and the shining have people chills Spook Baby always felt a sense of comfort and warmth. Why the name A Tablespoon Of Spook? ATSOS is a just a tablespoon of spook Spook Baby can offer from this vast ocean of a genre. Thus with all the inspiration and passion Spook Baby has built throughout the years, here's an ode to the world of Horror or Spook.
